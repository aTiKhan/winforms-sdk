Font Name: TelerikWebUI (Glyphs: 415)

TelerikWebUI font XAML glyph reference:

The font and the ResourceDictionary are integrated in the Telerik.Windows.Controls.dll
They are accessible in all our current themes in Development after merging the FontRerources.xaml from the Controls assembly via {StaticResource TelerikWebUI} and {StaticResource [GlyphKey]}.
<ResourceDictionary Source="/Telerik.Windows.Controls;component/Themes/FontResources.xaml"/>
Given that they are basically text, their color could be changed via setting Foreground.
The glyphs are designed at 16px height so they look better in sized multiple of 16.

Example usage:
<TextBlock FontFamily="{StaticResource TelerikWebUI}" FontSize="16" Text="{StaticResource GlyphPlus}" Foreground="Black" />
